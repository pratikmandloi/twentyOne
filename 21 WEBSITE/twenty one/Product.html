SHIPPING

HOME DELIVERY:

  • STANDARD DELIVERY - ₹ 290.00 / FREE (ORDERS OVER ₹ 2,990) The estimated delivery time will be between 2-8 working days, depending on the delivery address.

At the time of processing your purchase, we will show you the available shipping methods, the cost and the delivery date of your order.

EXCHANGES AND RETURNS

You have 30 days from the shipping date to return your purchase from TwentyOne.com.

The exchange option is not available for online purchases. Nevertheless, you can always return your order within the established return period and place another order.

  • AT A TwentyOne STORE
    You can return items at any TwentyOne store free of charge in the country/region where the purchase was made.
  • HOME COLLECTION
    You can request a home pick-up by going to the orders and returns section. Returns have a cost of ₹ 100 that will be deducted from the refunded amount.

Some of our items may have special conditions for returns. Further information in section Exchanges and Returns > Special Conditions.
